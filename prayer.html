PRAYER POINTS

  1. Father I thank you for the Holy Spirit that lives in me and I pray to yield to his ministry. 1 Corinthians 2:12
  2. Father I thank you for the miracle, signs and wonders, thank you for the answered prayers. 1 John 5:14-15
  3. Father I thank you because there is no enchantment or divination against me shall be establish. Number 23:23
  4. You said it is finished. Father I thank you because every problem, struggle, confusion, sickness, infirmities, cease from existing in the name of Jesus. John 19:30
  5. Isaiah 54:17. There is no weapon form fashion against me that will proper.
  6. John 3:14. I am life and it is the life of Christ am experiencing.
  7. Mark 9:23. Because I have the life and faith of God, there is nothing impossible for me.
  8. Isaiah 32:15. Father in the name of Jesus we declare according to your fulfilled promise, we see your spirit poured upon the church, we see the wilderness turns to fruitful field, we see the lives of people fruitful, productive. Thank you our heavenly father. Amen
  9. Isaiah 33:6. Father in the name of Jesus, we thank you for your wisdom and knowledge at work in us. We see and declare by wisdom and knowledge every member of the church are established in your will unmovable and unshakable in times of problem. Amen.
  10. Isaiah 32:18. Father in the name of Jesus, we declare according to your fulfilled promise every member of this church dwell in peaceable habitation. Your rest surround them. Amen
  11. Isaiah 37:35. Father in the name of Jesus, we thank you for you are our defense. You will continue to defend your church and save it. We are assured of your word, you are our strong tower, we will not be afraid. Amen
  12. Isaiah 41:18. Father in the name of Jesus, we thank you for your word fulfilled. We declare that rivers of your blessing, anointing, healing, abundance, salvation is following in our midst. We see dry land/life filled by these blessing and healing. Amen
  13. Isaiah 11:9. Father in the name of Jesus, we declare that your church is full of the knowledge of Christ even as the waters cover the sea.
  14. Isaiah 11:2. Father in the name of Jesus, we thank you for your spirit at work in our midst. We pray that the spirit of wisdom, understanding, the spirit of counsel and might, the spirit of knowledge and the fear of the Lord is in operation in our midst.
  15. Father in the name of Jesus, continue to destroy every blind-folding power of the enemy aimed at preventing multitudes from getting saved and established in this church. 2 Corinthians 4 : 4
  16. Father in the name of Jesus, let the Holy Ghost continue to brood over the heart of men, thereby causing men to be drawn to Jesus and be saved. 1 Corinthian 12 : 3
  17. Father in the name of Jesus, let the ears of every unsaved soul across our church and environs continue to hear the compelling sound of the Holy Spirit and be drafted to the cross and be saved. Isaiah 5 : 26
  18. Father in the name of Jesus, let your fear fall on every unrepented sinner in our church and the environs, bringing them to the feet of the cross and the Christ glorified in them. Act 19 : 17
  19. Father in the name of Jesus, let your word mightily grow in CHECOMI and the environs and let it prevail in the soul men. Act 19 : 20
  20. Father in the name of Jesus, we come against all satanic interferences that might hinder souls from being saved and establish in Christ. 1 John 3 : 8
  21. Father in the name of Jesus, let your reaper-angels go across the home of every unsaved members and unsaved people in the environs, bringing them visions and dreams that will lead them to Christ. Acts 10 : 1 – 4
  22. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray as many members of CHECOMI still sitting in darkness and in the region of the shadow of death, we declare they begin to see the light of Christ. Matthew 4 : 15 – 16
  23. Isaiah 27:6. Father in the name of Jesus, we declare that every member of CHECOMI is rooted in Christ. They blossom, bud and are fruitful in Jesus name.
  24. Ephesian 3:17. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray that Christ dwell in the heart of every member of CHECOMI. They are rooted and grounded in the love of Christ. Amen
  25. Philippian 1:9. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray that the love of Christ in CHECOMI abound you more and more. Everyone is filled with the fruits of righteousness. Amen
  26. Ephesians 6:10 – 11. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray that every member is clothed with your whole armor, strengthening them to stand for you.
  27. Colossian 1:10. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray that every member by your spirit will walk worthy of you unto all pleasing, being fruitful in every good work and increase in the knowledge of God.
  28. Ephesians 4:13. Father in the name of Jesus, we declare that by your gifting’s bring made manifested in your church CHECOMI, every member come into the unity of faith and in the knowledge of God, being made perfect and coming into the full measure of Christ.
  29. Galatian 4:19. Father in the name of Jesus, we travail today, declaring that Christ be formed in us, in your church CHECOMI, bringing unprecedented spiritual growth.
  30. 2 Thessalonian 3:5. Father in the name of Jesus, we pray that the hearts of every church member be directed into the love of God, faith and hope arise in everyone. Amen
