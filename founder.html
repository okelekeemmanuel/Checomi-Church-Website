Welcome address from the General Overseer of CHECOMI

Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ!

I

t is with great joy and gratitude that I welcome you to our church website as the General Overseer of this blessed congregation. As you embark on this virtual journey, allow me to extend my heartfelt welcome to you, whether you are a long-standing member, a curious seeker, or a visitor exploring the teachings and values we hold dear.

Our church is a vibrant community of believers who gather to worship, learn, and grow in our faith. We are committed to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ in our daily lives, seeking to embody love, compassion, and service to others. We firmly believe in the power of unity, where we celebrate our diversity, embracing individuals from all backgrounds, cultures, and walks of life.

In this ever-evolving digital age, our church website serves as a gateway to connect with our congregation and share the timeless message of God's love, compassion, and grace. Here, you will find a wealth of resources, upcoming events, and opportunities to get involved in our ministries. We strive to offer inspiring sermons, insightful teachings, and transformative worship experiences that will empower you to deepen your relationship with God.

As the General Overseer, I am privileged to lead a team of dedicated clergy, committed staff, and passionate volunteers. Together, we are here to support you on your spiritual journey, providing guidance, pastoral care, and prayerful support. We believe in the importance of nurturing a community where individuals feel seen, heard, and loved, knowing that through our collective efforts, we can make a positive difference in our church, our local community, and beyond.

I invite you to explore our website, engage with our online community, and discover ways to connect with us. I encourage you to participate in worship services, educational programs, and outreach initiatives as we seek to serve and uplift one another. Our church is not just a physical space but a family, a sanctuary where you can find solace, companionship, and a deeper understanding of God's purpose for your life.

Once again, I extend a warm welcome to you, and I pray that as you navigate our website, you encounter the love of Christ and the transformative power of His grace. May this online platform serve as a bridge that connects us, inspires us, and empowers us to be the hands and feet of Jesus in the world.

May God bless you abundantly, and may His presence be with you always.

Yours in Christ,

Rev. R. A Adesanmi

General Overseer
