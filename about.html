Welcome to Christ Healing Covenant Ministry Internatiol

Connect, Grow and Serve with Us

"A vibrant and growing Christian Pentecostal church located in Ogun state. Our church was founded in 1999 by R.A Adesanmi (Mrs), with a mission to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ and make meaningful impact in the lives of individuals and families within our community and beyond.

At CHECOMI, we believe in the power of prayer, the inspiration of the Bible, the anointing of the Holy Spirit, and the transformation of lives through the grace of God. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and engaging worship experience for everyone who enters our doors, whether you are a long-time believer or are just starting to explore questions of faith.

Our pastor (R.A Adesanmi) is a dynamic and passionate leader who has dedicated her life to serving God and sharing Her love with others. Our ministerial team is made up of ordained ministers and licensed pastors who are trained, dedicated, and caring. We offer a range of programs and services for all age groups, including Sunday worship services, Bible studies, youth fellowships, women's and men's fellowships, prayer meetings, and more.

We invite you to come and join us at CHECOMI as we pursue our vision of being a center of spiritual and social transformation in our community and beyond. Feel free to explore our website for more information, and if you have any questions or need any assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to us."
