The Heart of Praise

Speaker: Rev R.A Adesanmi Text: ISAIAH 43 VS 21 On Sunday 13 Jan, 2019

The most important reason of man creation is so that we can praise our creator. A heart of praise is a heart of gratitude and a heart that value God.

Things to note:

  • A heart of praise is a grateful and thankful heart. Psalm 9:1, Psalm 105:1
  • A heart flowing with worship and adoration of the almightiness of God. Psalm 96:4

Fear Thou Not

Speaker: Rev R.A Adesanmi Text: ISAIAH 41:10 On Monday 12 Sept, 2022

INTRODUCTION: The Spirit of fear is the spirit that make someone to be panic. The spirit fear is an evil spirit. Revelation 21:8. Anyone that has this kind of spirit, is in the bondage of Satan, darkness and an unclean spirit.

Thing to know about fear

  • Fear is the tool/ instrument of the devil, he strikes first with fear
  • Fear is the opposite of faith:
    • it is believing that devil is powerful than God
    • it is trusting in the power of God
  • Fear amplify or add or increase and magnify challenges. For God has not given us the spirit of fear, but of power, love and of a sound mind. II Timothy 1:7

Power of Prayer

Speaker: Rev R.A Adesanmi Text: Psalm 5:1-3, On Sunday 22 Jan, 2023

This passage opens our mind of understanding and knowledge about God we are serving. He is a God who has ear to listening to our prayers. You need to pray in order for you to be promoted from where you are to where you are supposed to be. You must pray so that your spiritual eyes will shine bright and brighter. You need to pray to come closer to God You should pray the prayer of the righteous that avails much

EXAMPLES OF PEOPLE THAT PRAYED IN THE BIBLE:

  • Abraham prayed and Lot was saved from being destroyed in Sodom. Genesis 14:1-end, Genesis 19:12-end

I got my second admission to school with the aim of studying MICRO-BIOLOGY but I was giving PHYSICS, I was very angry, I then receive a call from my mother in the Lord that that is the course I was met to study and I graduated successfully without any carry-over in all my semester. Help me to praise my faithful father.

Miss Boluwatife Esther.

Praise the Lord. I give thanks to God Almighty for His protection upon me and my family, GOD saved us from a critical and life threatening accident on our way from Ibadan to Lagos, our car somersaulted three times before it stopped, but I and my family came out without any major bruises on our body. All thanks to GOD that give us the privilege to journey again in this year.

Miss Ayomide Adesanya.

Appreciation to God who gave me the privilege to enter into an institution and secondly, I give thanks to God Almighty for His protection, He did not allow an evil bird to kill me. I was on my way coming from school when this bird flew and stood on my head. Immediately I came back from school I made use of the anointing oil we do pray on in church, I fell sick the week after, but all thanks to God who saved me from this evil bird. Praise the lord!!

Miss Damilola Oladipupo

I give glory, honour and adoration to God almighty for his protection upon my son. He called on the night cross over that he was saved from a critical accident, he said he wanted to enter a bus but left the bus and entered another bus because of an added N50 to the fare, unknowingly to him, he didn’t know that he was being led by the Spirit of God, on his way he saw the bus involved in an accident, people were injured while some died. he got home that same night, and found a snake in his room and he killed it. Glory be to God Almighty for HIS protection upon my son and family.

Mrs Oladipupo

I give thanks to God who did not allow the bird of this world to use me and my family as a prey. There was a particular bird that normal fly at the roof of my old house which we did not think of it as anything, with the blessings of the Lord we change our apartment and this same bird came flying again with weird sound, then I and my family started prayer, to my biggest surprise, this bird flew to the apartment we packed away from and died there. Praise God for his protection.

Mrs Adeniyi.

I give thanks to God for his faithfulness upon my life and education aspect. I have been processing an admission for two year in FUTA with nothing to show for it. I have even done other JAMB but glory be to OUR Faithful Father, I was given admission three weeks into the school semester when I myself have lost hope thinking of trying again. Praise the name of the Lord.

Mr Aladeokomo Adeniyi
